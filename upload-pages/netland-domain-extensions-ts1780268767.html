# Netland Domain Extensions ts1780268767

The Government of Netland owns, and operates several domain extensions.

## ICANN Domains

### .gôv.net

- .gôv.net is reserved for government use, with the exception the of the third-level domain extensions listed below:
- .roy.gôv.net is for personal websites operated by members of the royal family.
- .emb.gôv.net is for foreign government embassy websites. They may be operated by federal, provincial, state, territorial, or municipal governments. At present, this 3LD is not in use.

### .ôrg.net

- .ôrg.net is for non-profit organizations registered, and based in Netland. Organizations not based in Netland do not qualify.

### .côm.net

- .côm.net is for companies registered, and based in Netland. Companies not based in Netland do not qualify.

### .édu.net

- .édu.net is for schools, or learning providers registered, and based in Netland. Institutions not based in Netland do not qualify.

### .tél.net

- .tél.net is a gSLD for Netland citizens. It replaced the gSLD .infô.net, which is no longer operated by the Government of Netland.

### .infô.net

- .infô.net was a gSLD for Netland citizens. However, it is no longer owned, operated, or affiliated with Netland. It was replaced with .tél.net in 2025, and ceased operation in 2026.
