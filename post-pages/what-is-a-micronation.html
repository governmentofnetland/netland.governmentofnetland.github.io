What Is A Micronation

Date Created:May 31, 2026
Date Published:May 31, 2026
Date Last Updated:May 31, 2026
1


Anyone can create a new country, but unless it is recognized by existing widely recognized nations it is generally termed a micronation. While some micronations are somewhat serious attempts to create new independent countries, the majority tend to be made as jokes, hobbies, or attractions. They are often short-lived. In some cases lasting only a few weeks. They also generally have little, or no legitimacy to their claims of being independent nations.
